About The Retro Bee

Based in Hitchin, Hertfordshire, Steve Cash created 'The Retro Bee' - enveloping both the focus of the company and his Mancunian roots - in 2019, after retiring from his previous job where he had been working for thirty years. Not wanting to simply sit at home all day, and knowing that he had both a long-standing love and a keen eye for mid-century furniture, Steve began buying and restoring the odd sideboard from Freecycle. This quickly developed into more than a hobby, and he soon decided to turn his passion into something concrete, building a shed at the top of his garden to house and photograph the pieces that came his way. In the present-day, The Retro Bee delives both nationally and internationally, has 20,000 Instagram followers, and has been featured in various media such as Reclaim magazine.
